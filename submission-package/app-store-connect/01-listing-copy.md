## Description (4000 characters max)

```
FilmContract is the all-in-one platform where independent film producers and actors connect, collaborate, and get paid — securely.

FOR PRODUCERS:
Post casting calls and reach verified actors instantly. Review audition submissions in a streamlined pipeline. When you find the right talent, create legally-structured contracts and process payments through secure escrow — all without leaving the app.

FOR ACTORS:
Browse open casting calls matched to your profile. Submit audition tapes directly to producers. Track your applications in real time and know the moment you're shortlisted or hired. Sign contracts digitally and receive payments through protected escrow.

SECURE PAYMENTS:
Every transaction flows through Stripe-powered escrow. Funds are held safely until both parties confirm delivery. No more chasing checks or worrying about payment disputes.

PROFESSIONAL CONTRACTS:
Choose from industry-standard contract templates or create custom agreements. Both parties sign digitally with full audit trails. Every contract is stored and accessible anytime.

BUILT-IN MESSAGING:
Communicate directly with producers or talent. Discuss project details, negotiate terms, and coordinate schedules — all within FilmContract.

KEY FEATURES:
• Post and browse casting calls with role details, budgets, and deadlines
• Submit and review audition tapes
• Create, send, and sign contracts digitally
• Secure escrow payments powered by Stripe Connect
• Real-time messaging between producers and actors
• Actor profiles with headshots, bios, reels, and portfolio photos
• Producer profiles with company info and project history
• Push notifications for submissions, hires, and payments
• Dark mode support
• Works on iPhone and iPad

FilmContract charges a 7.5% platform fee on completed transactions. No monthly subscriptions. No hidden costs. You only pay when money moves.

Whether you're casting your next short film or auditioning for your breakout role, FilmContract gives you the professional tools to make it happen.
```
