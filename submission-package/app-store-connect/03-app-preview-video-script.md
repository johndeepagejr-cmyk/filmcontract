# App Store Connect — App Preview Video Script

## Specifications

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Duration | 15–30 seconds |
| Resolution | 1290 × 2796 (iPhone 6.7") |
| Format | H.264, .mov or .mp4 |
| Frame rate | 30 fps |
| Audio | Optional (background music recommended, no voiceover required) |

## Script — 30 Seconds, 8 Scenes

### Scene 1 — Opening (0:00–0:03)
**Visual:** FilmContract logo animates in on a dark navy background. Tagline fades in below.
**Text overlay:** "FilmContract — Where Film Gets Done"
**Transition:** Fade to app

### Scene 2 — Actor Browses Casting Calls (0:03–0:07)
**Visual:** Actor Home screen. Thumb scrolls through casting call cards. One card is tapped.
**Text overlay:** "Discover casting calls"
**Action:** Scroll feed → Tap a casting call card

### Scene 3 — Casting Call Detail & Submit (0:07–0:11)
**Visual:** Casting call detail screen loads. Camera pans down to show role details, budget, deadline. Thumb taps "Submit Audition."
**Text overlay:** "Submit your audition"
**Action:** View details → Tap Submit button

### Scene 4 — Track Submissions (0:11–0:14)
**Visual:** My Submissions screen showing 3 submissions with status badges (Submitted, Shortlisted, Hired).
**Text overlay:** "Track every application"
**Action:** Quick scroll through submission list

### Scene 5 — Producer Dashboard (0:14–0:18)
**Visual:** Switch to Producer view. Dashboard with stats cards (3 Active Contracts, $12,500 Pending, 2 Casting Calls). Quick actions grid visible.
**Text overlay:** "Manage your production"
**Action:** Show dashboard → Tap "Post Casting"

### Scene 6 — Create Contract (0:18–0:22)
**Visual:** Contract creation flow. Producer fills in project name, selects actor, sets amount. Taps "Send Contract."
**Text overlay:** "Professional contracts in seconds"
**Action:** Fill form → Tap Send

### Scene 7 — Secure Payment (0:22–0:26)
**Visual:** Escrow payment screen showing funds held, release confirmation. Green checkmark animation on completion.
**Text overlay:** "Payments protected by escrow"
**Action:** Show escrow status → Release funds → Success animation

### Scene 8 — Closing (0:26–0:30)
**Visual:** Split screen showing actor profile on left, producer dashboard on right. Both fade to FilmContract logo.
**Text overlay:** "Download FilmContract — Free"
**Transition:** Fade to logo + App Store badge

## Recording Instructions

1. Use **QuickTime Player** → File → New Screen Recording on macOS with the iPhone 15 Pro Max simulator
2. Alternatively, use a physical device: Settings → Control Center → Screen Recording
3. Record each scene separately, then edit together in **iMovie**, **Final Cut Pro**, or **CapCut**
4. Add text overlays in the editor (white text, SF Pro Display Bold, drop shadow)
5. Background music: Use a royalty-free cinematic track from [Artlist](https://artlist.io) or [Epidemic Sound](https://epidemicsound.com)
6. Export at 1290 × 2796, H.264, 30fps

## Music Recommendations

Search for "cinematic ambient" or "inspiring technology" tracks. The tone should feel professional, aspirational, and modern. Avoid anything too dramatic or heavy — the app is a business tool, not a movie trailer.
