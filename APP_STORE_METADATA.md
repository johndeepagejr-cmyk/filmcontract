## App Store Description (4000 chars max)

FilmContract is the all-in-one platform for film and entertainment professionals. Whether you're an actor looking for your next role or a producer managing talent and contracts, FilmContract streamlines every step — from casting calls to signed contracts to secure payments.

**FOR ACTORS:**

Discover casting opportunities tailored to your profile. Browse open casting calls by role type, production category, and compensation. Submit professional self-tapes with our broadcast-quality recorder featuring a built-in teleprompter, multiple takes, and quality presets. Track your submissions in real-time and get notified instantly when you're shortlisted, called back, or hired.

- Browse and filter casting calls (film, TV, commercial, voice over)
- Record self-tapes with professional camera controls and teleprompter
- Edit self-tapes with trim, slate overlay, and compression tools
- Track submission status from applied to hired
- Receive secure escrow payments directly in-app
- Build your digital portfolio with headshots, reels, and credits

**FOR PRODUCERS:**

Post casting calls in minutes with our 4-step wizard. Review self-tape submissions with side-by-side comparison, star ratings, and quick tags. Manage your talent pipeline from submission to hire with a Kanban-style workflow. When you find the right actor, hire them directly into a contract with pre-filled details.

- Create detailed casting calls with multi-role support
- Review submissions with comparison mode and rating rubrics
- Manage applicant pipeline (Submitted → Reviewed → Shortlisted → Callback → Hired)
- Generate professional contracts with customizable templates
- Secure escrow payments protect both parties
- Real-time messaging with talent

**CONTRACTS & PAYMENTS:**

Create, negotiate, and sign contracts digitally. Our escrow payment system holds funds securely until work is completed, protecting both actors and producers. Track earnings, payment history, and dispute resolution all in one place.

- Digital contract creation with industry templates
- E-signature support for binding agreements
- Escrow payments with milestone support
- Dispute resolution workflow
- Complete payment history and earnings dashboard

**STAY CONNECTED:**

Real-time push notifications keep you in the loop. Get alerted when contracts are sent, submissions are reviewed, payments are released, or messages arrive. Never miss an opportunity.

Download FilmContract today and join the modern way to work in film and entertainment.
