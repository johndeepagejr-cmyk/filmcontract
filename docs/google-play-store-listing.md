## Full Description (4000 characters max)

FilmContract is the premier mobile platform for managing film industry contracts between actors and producers. Whether you're an independent filmmaker hiring talent or an actor reviewing your next role, FilmContract streamlines the entire contract lifecycle from creation to digital signing and payment processing.

**For Producers:**
Create professional contracts using industry-standard templates including SAG-AFTRA Standard Agreements, Non-Union Actor Agreements, Day Player Contracts, and Extra/Background Agreements. Browse verified actor profiles, review portfolios, and send contracts directly through the app. Track contract status, manage payments, and maintain a complete record of all your production agreements.

**For Actors:**
Discover production opportunities, showcase your portfolio with photos and experience, and review contracts on your phone. Digitally sign agreements with legally binding e-signatures powered by HelloSign. Track your contract history, manage payments, and build your professional reputation with verified reviews and ratings.

**Key Features:**
• Contract Templates — Industry-standard templates for SAG-AFTRA, non-union, day player, and background performer agreements
• Digital Signatures — Legally binding e-signatures with HelloSign integration and signature certificates
• Actor & Producer Profiles — Verified profiles with portfolios, ratings, and contract history
• Payment Processing — Secure payment handling with Stripe integration for contract compensation
• Contract Review — Review and approve contracts with full terms, compensation details, and production schedules
• Analytics Dashboard — Track your contracts, earnings, and professional activity
• Push Notifications — Stay updated on contract status changes, new opportunities, and payment confirmations
• Privacy & Security — Enterprise-grade encryption and secure document storage

FilmContract is designed specifically for the entertainment industry, with features tailored to the unique needs of film production contracts. Our platform ensures that every agreement is professional, legally compliant, and easy to manage from your mobile device.

Download FilmContract today and take control of your film industry contracts.
