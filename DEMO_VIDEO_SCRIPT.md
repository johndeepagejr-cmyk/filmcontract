**Key Message:** The Industry's First Dedicated Film Contract Mobile App

**Target Platform:** App Store and Google Play Store

## Overview

This script guides the creation of a professional demo video showcasing FilmContract's key features. The video should appeal to both producers and actors by highlighting how the app streamlines contract management, payment tracking, and professional networking.

## Video Structure

The demo video follows a narrative arc that introduces the problem, presents the solution, and demonstrates key features. The total runtime is 2 minutes 30 seconds, optimized for mobile viewing and app store requirements.

## Scene 1: Problem Introduction (0:00 - 0:15)

**Visual**: Show a producer and actor struggling with paperwork, emails, and spreadsheets. Use split-screen to show both perspectives.

**Voiceover**: "Managing contracts between producers and actors has always been complicated. Endless emails, scattered documents, and unclear payment terms create confusion and delays."

**On-Screen Text**: "The Problem"

**Music**: Upbeat, modern background music starts playing at low volume.

## Scene 2: Solution Introduction (0:15 - 0:30)

**Visual**: Transition to the FilmContract app logo with smooth animation. Show the app icon and title.

**Voiceover**: "Introducing FilmContract - transparent contracts for film professionals. A mobile app that simplifies contract creation, payment tracking, and professional networking."

**On-Screen Text**: "FilmContract - Transparent Contracts for Film Professionals"

**Music**: Music volume increases slightly.

## Scene 3: Login and Onboarding (0:30 - 0:45)

**Visual**: Show the login screen with "Sign In with Manus" button. Tap the button and show the onboarding tutorial appearing with interactive tooltips.

**Voiceover**: "Getting started is simple. Sign in with your account and follow the interactive tutorial to learn about key features."

**On-Screen Text**: "Easy Setup"

**UI Elements**: Highlight the onboarding tutorial with animated arrows pointing to key features.

## Scene 4: Contract Creation (0:45 - 1:00)

**Visual**: Navigate to the Create tab and show the contract creation flow. Fill in producer name, actor name, role, payment amount, and dates. Tap "Create Contract".

**Voiceover**: "Create contracts in seconds. Specify the producer, actor, role, payment amount, and shooting dates. The app handles the rest."

**On-Screen Text**: "Create Contracts Instantly"

**UI Elements**: Show form fields being filled with smooth animations.

## Scene 5: Payment Tracking (1:00 - 1:15)

**Visual**: Navigate to the contract detail screen and show the payment timeline. Display recorded payments with dates and amounts. Show the payment progress bar indicating how much has been paid.

**Voiceover**: "Track payments effortlessly. Record payments as they're made and see your payment history at a glance. The app reminds you of pending payments automatically."

**On-Screen Text**: "Smart Payment Tracking"

**UI Elements**: Animate the payment timeline and progress bar to show updates.

## Scene 6: Portfolio Management (1:15 - 1:30)

**Visual**: Navigate to the Profile tab and show the portfolio section. Display portfolio photos in the grid layout. Tap to show the portfolio theme selector with different layout options (grid, masonry, carousel).

**Voiceover**: "Actors can showcase their work with customizable portfolios. Choose from grid, masonry, or carousel layouts to display your best photos and videos."

**On-Screen Text**: "Beautiful Portfolios"

**UI Elements**: Show smooth transitions between different portfolio layouts.

## Scene 7: Directory and Favorites (1:30 - 1:45)

**Visual**: Navigate to the Producers tab and show the directory of producers. Long-press a producer card to show the quick actions menu. Tap the heart icon to add to favorites. Navigate to the Favorites section in the profile.

**Voiceover**: "Find and connect with industry professionals. Browse producers and actors, save your favorites, and access quick actions for faster workflows."

**On-Screen Text**: "Connect with Professionals"

## Scene 8: Analytics Dashboard (1:45 - 2:00)

**Voiceover**: "Get insights into your performance. Track portfolio views, contract trends, and payment history with beautiful visual charts. Make data-driven decisions."

## Scene 9: Verification and Trust (2:00 - 2:15)

**Voiceover**: "Build trust in the community. Verified profiles and trust scores based on contract completion and performance help professionals find reliable partners."

**Voiceover**: "FilmContract - Making film contracts simple, transparent, and fair. Download today and start managing your contracts like a pro."

## Production Notes

### Recording Guidelines

**Device**: Record on an iPhone 14 Pro or similar high-end smartphone for best quality. Use a tripod or stand to keep the phone steady.

**Screen Recording**: Use the built-in screen recording feature (iOS: Control Center > Screen Recording, Android: Settings > Advanced > Screen Recorder).

**Lighting**: Record in a well-lit environment with natural light or professional lighting to ensure clear visibility of the app interface.

**Audio**: Record voiceover in a quiet environment using a quality microphone. Use audio editing software to adjust levels and remove background noise.

### Editing Guidelines

**Transitions**: Use smooth fade transitions between scenes (0.5 second duration). Avoid jarring cuts or overly complex transitions.

**Text Overlays**: Use clear, readable fonts (sans-serif like Helvetica or Arial). Keep text on screen for at least 2 seconds for viewers to read.

**Music**: Use royalty-free background music from libraries like Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or YouTube Audio Library. Keep music at a level that doesn't overpower the voiceover.

**Voiceover**: Speak clearly and at a moderate pace. Use professional voiceover talent or high-quality text-to-speech software for better quality.

**Color Grading**: Apply subtle color grading to match the app's brand colors and create a cohesive visual style.

### Technical Specifications

**Resolution**: Export video in 1080p (1920x1080) or 4K (3840x2160) for best quality on modern devices.

**Frame Rate**: Use 30fps or 60fps depending on the editing software and platform requirements.

**Aspect Ratio**: Use 9:16 (portrait) for mobile app store videos, or 16:9 (landscape) for web and marketing use.

**File Format**: Export as MP4 (H.264 codec) for maximum compatibility across platforms.

**File Size**: Keep file size under 500MB for easy uploading to app stores.
